VACCINATION

Vaccinations help prevent your dog catching and spreading some very serious infectious diseases, many of which can be fatal. When a dog is vaccinated, a small amount of the disease is injected into your dog, giving their body a chance to learn how to destroy it. This means that if they encounter the real disease, they are much less likely to become unwell because they are ready to fight it.
